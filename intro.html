How Did Basketball Start?

Basketball is a popular team sport that has become one of the most beloved and widely played games across the globe. It was created in December 1891 by the Canadian physical education instructor James Naismith, who was looking for a fun indoor activity to keep his students active during the winter months. Since then, basketball has become a global phenomenon, featuring in schoolyards, playgrounds and stadiums around the world.

The game’s popularity is driven by its fast pace, athleticism and excitement. Two teams of five players each compete to score points by shooting a ball through a 10-foot-high hoop (the basket) on a rectangular court. Players use their hands to pass, dribble and shoot the ball, while trying to disrupt their opponents’ plans using a combination of physicality and strategic gameplay. The game can be played both indoors and outdoors, and different variations of the sport have emerged over time, such as wheelchair basketball, street ball and 3-on-3.
